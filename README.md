# Raritone Summer Internship 2026 – Data Analytics Internship

## Overview

This repository contains all the work completed during the **Raritone Summer Internship 2026** as a **Data Analyst Intern**. The internship focused on analyzing customer behavior, user engagement, retention strategies, conversion funnels, fashion e-commerce trends, competitor research, business intelligence, and virtual try-on analytics.

The objective was to use data-driven insights to understand user behavior and provide recommendations to improve customer engagement, retention, and conversion rates for Raritone.

---

## Company Overview

**Raritone** is a fashion-tech company that leverages **Virtual Try-On technology** to enhance online shopping experiences. The platform helps users visualize clothing before purchase, increasing confidence and reducing uncertainty in fashion e-commerce.

---

## Internship Objectives

* Understand customer behavior in fashion e-commerce.
* Analyze user engagement and retention metrics.
* Study virtual try-on adoption and usage patterns.
* Research fashion shopping trends and competitor strategies.
* Build analytics dashboards and reports.
* Generate business intelligence insights and recommendations.

## Key Findings

* Virtual Try-On significantly improves purchase confidence.
* Personalized recommendations increase user engagement.
* Wishlist users demonstrate higher retention rates.
* Social media strongly influences fashion purchase decisions.
* Customer segmentation improves targeting and personalization.
* AI-powered recommendations enhance product discovery.

---

## Recommendations for Raritone

1. Enhance AI-powered recommendation systems.
2. Increase visibility of virtual try-on features.
3. Implement loyalty and rewards programs.
4. Use personalized notifications and reminders.
5. Introduce AI styling and complete-the-look suggestions.
6. Improve customer segmentation for targeted marketing.
7. Continue investing in virtual try-on innovation.
